Speedmarks

What's up with Speedmarks?

Starring

Sometimes once isn't enough. You may actually want to check out the same website many times and constantly re-Speedmarking the page is so last year.

Click the star icon next to any link to permanently keep a Speedmark so you can look at it again and again and again. We won't get rid of it until you un-star it and THEN click it.

Sharing

Your Share Name

If you want to start sharing your Speedmarks with your friends, you first have to get a sharing name.

A sharing name helps your friends easily identify you while still keeping your Speedmarks a really cool secret.

To get a share name, click Options in the lower right corner of your Speedmarks page. Then, click enable sharing. Your fun new share name will then appear below!

You don't have to have a share name. Just ignore the "Options" link. I won't judge you either way.

Sharing a Speedmark

Sharing is easy. If you want to share a Speedmark with a friend, follow these easy steps:

  1. Get your friend's share name. It might be something like ChunkyBacon.
  2. Click the green share icon next to the Speedmark that you want to share.
  3. Enter your friend's share name in the box.
  4. Click share.
  5. Relax in a hammock. You've had a long day today.
